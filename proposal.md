# **Title - Godzilla Defense Force Clicker!**


## **Repository**
<https://github.com/Mayamorales-327/MoralesMaya_PFDA_Final_Project>

## Description
Use planes to attack Godzilla by clicking! But the more you click attack, the stronger Godzilla gets!

## Features
- Feature 1 - **Evolving Godzilla**
	-  After the player clicks a certain amount of times, the Godzilla sprite on the screen will evolve to multiple forms, making it stronger and requiring more clicks to attack. 

Form 1 - Base Godzilla 
Form 2 - Godzilla with quicker attacks (sparking glowing spikes)
Form 3 Godzilla with fully glowing spikes and atomic breath

- Feature 2 - **Upgrading Player Attacks**
	-  After a certain amount of clicks, the player will be able to upgrade their planes to attack the higher level forms that Godzilla evolves too. 

Stronger planes 
Stronger missiles 
Quicker attack speed

- Feature 3 - **Save Data**
	- Making players able to save their game data locally on their computer
